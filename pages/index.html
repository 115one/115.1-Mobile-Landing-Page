115.1 Messenger

A private messenger for disciples of Jesus.

Screenshots

Description

Our mission is to provide a secure suite of digital tools to help disciples of Jesus connect with each other and their supporters globally, without risk of personal attack or restrictions on their freedom of speech.

Make group voice and video calls with up to 8 people on iOS, Android, and desktop. All calls are made over a Wi-Fi or data connection and (with the exception of data fees) are free of charge, including long-distance and international.

Security features include:

  • State-of-the-art encryption – messages and calls cannot be accessed by us or other third parties because they are always end-to-end encrypted, private, and secure.
  • Secure servers – the backend is powered by our own servers that encrypt your data. No one can read your messages or listen to your calls, not even us.
  • Safe photo sharing – we automatically strip the metadata that could reveal your location. There is even a tool to blur the faces of people in your photographs.
  • Disappearing messages – which are automatically deleted after a specified period of time, with no history or trace that they ever existed.
